Computer Says Yes, Overcoming Tech Helplessness

22 May 2019

INTERACTIVE: Click, drag and release the red ball in the box

This is a reminder, tech is what we want it to be.

Program a digital rubber band to send a bouncy ball around a box on your screen, because, why not.

Design open digital tools to make information accessible and increase public trust in the planning system, because, it is a better way to do things.

Code restrictions to ensure autonomous vehicles don’t result in cities with unfathomable sprawl and streets a no-go zone for people, because, we want good places to live.

Tech can be frustrating but ultimately, we have much more control in how we design it than almost anything else in our lives. Let's make it work for us.
